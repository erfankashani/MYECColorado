About MYECColorado

Founded in 2016, the Muslim Youth Empowerment Conference (MYEC) engages, empowers, and evolves Muslim youth in Colorado through spiritual, academic, and professional development.

MYECColorado 2019

We are calling all Muslim youth (age 6th grade to college) in Colorado to join us the 2019 MYEC!

Join us for a day-conference focused on engaging, empowering, and evolving Muslim youth! The conference is free admission (thanks to our generous sponsors!), and lunch will be provided. This year's conference will feature phenomenal keynote speakers, workshops, networking opportunities, and afternoon activities!

Event Details: The 2019 MYEC is scheduled for Saturday, December 21, 2019 from 9 AM to 6 PM in the CU Student Commons Building, 1201 Larimer St, Denver, CO 80204. Muslim youth please register below via the Youth Registration link. There will also be a Parent Workshop from 3-6 PM during the conference, and more information can be found through the Parent Workshop Registration link below.
